Hearthfire Harvest's Soup

A vegetarian soup with fresh produce, perfect for a quiet evening by the hearth.

Recipe:

  1. Sauté onions, leeks, and garlic until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, celery, and sweet bell peppers.
  3. Pour in vegetable stock and bring to a boil.
  4. Add Skyrim herbs like lavender and sage.
  5. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  6. Stir in a dollop of cream and a sprinkle of cheese before serving.
  7. Serve with herb-infused flatbread.
