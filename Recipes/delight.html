Dragonborn's Delight

A sumptuous pie with spiced beef, sweetfire root, and cheese, encased in flaky pastry.

  1. Prepare flaky pastry dough and chill.
  2. Cook ground beef with onions, garlic, and Skyrim spices like sweetfire root and frost salts.
  3. Mix in shredded cheese and cool.
  4. Roll out the pastry dough and fill with the beef mixture.
  5. Cover with another pastry layer, sealing edges.
  6. Brush with beaten egg and bake until golden.
  7. Serve in wedges with steamed cabbage.
